MICRONEEDLING

Microneedling is a minimally invasive dermaroller procedure that uses very fine needles to create microscopic pricks in the skin. This process rejuvenates the skin and improves the look of scars, boosts collagen, and even encourages hair growth.

  • Brown Spots
  • Stretch Marks
  • Fine Lines / Wrinkles
  • Pigment Issues
  • Acne Scarring
  • Pore Size
  • Skin Texture
  • Aging

PRP (PLATELET - RICH PLASMA)

During the PRP Process your own blood's platelets are injected into the treated area(s). This process is commonly used by athletes to treat injuries and can also help with those suffering from the physical and mental effects of thinning hair.

  • Thinning Hair
  • Torn Ligaments
  • Damaged / Torn Muscles
  • Broken Bones
  • Inflamed / Torn Tendon
  • Cartilage Injury
